## Notes on the special cases
- **Monarch Bay bayside holes (1, 9, 16–18):** use the USGS **1 m seamless DEM** instead of the
  0.4 m LiDAR because USGS collected no ground LiDAR over the bay/marsh — still public domain,
  just lower resolution. Honest, documented in the book's metadata.
- **Poppy Ridge:** the *yardage cards* (scorecard facts + blank greens) are clean. The *aerial
  reference* was rebuilt from **USDA NAIP (public domain)** on 2026‑07‑13 (Esri/Maxar imagery
  removed); it shows the **pre‑2025 (old) layout** and is labeled as such — site/area reference only.
- **Jay Blasi routing diagram (Poppy):** only **viewed** to try to number holes; **never
  reproduced or embedded** in any output. Extracting factual hole positions is not copying.
