3. **Weak (nominative) — course names / depicting a private club.** Handled by nominative use
   + non‑affiliation + removal‑on‑request.
4. **Weak, mitigated — "Designed to conform · Rule 4.3" + no‑warranty.** Free product;

5. **Frivolous — viewing the Jay Blasi routing diagram** (never copied/embedded).
6. **Clean — OSM/USGS licensing.**

   output. Left as‑is (altering upstream provenance is worse).
